How to Play Laprecta

Learn the rules and flow of the game.

Goal

Be the first player to complete the agreed number of laps around the board.

Turn Structure

• Draw a Dice card
• Move forward or backward
• Resolve the space you land on
• Play Bless/Curse cards if allowed
• Add a new space to extend the track

Card Types

• Safe
• Bless
• Curse
• Checkpoint
• Start/Finish
