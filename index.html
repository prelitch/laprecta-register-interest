Laprecta

A racing strategy game where you build the track as you play.

What is Laprecta?

Laprecta is a competitive lap‑racing strategy game where players build the track as they move through it. Each turn adds new spaces, triggers Bless or Curse effects, and shifts momentum through forward or backward movement.

Prototype in development — not final.

Track Building

Players draw and place Space Cards to build the track dynamically. No two games are ever the same.

Bless & Curse Effects

Tactical twists that shift momentum, create timing windows, and reward clever planning.

Shared Strategy

Laprecta is built for players who enjoy clear rules, tactical movement, and shared‑board strategy.

Dynamic Movement

Dice cards push players forward or backward each turn, creating constant decision‑making moments.
